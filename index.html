What Is MediHarmony?

MediHarmony is a helpful tool that allows you to discover more information about your medicine and potential illnesses. We have two tools, the MediFinder and the MediBot. The MediFinder allows you to locate nearby pharmacies, so that you can easily get your medicine. The MediBot is an interactive chatbot. You can ask questions to the bot about illnesses and diseases.

What Is MediFinder?

MediFinder can give you important information about hospitals that are nearby. It will find your location and mark nearby hospitals, so you can find them easily.

What Is MediBot?

MediBot can give you info about the latest and most deadly diseases, it will tell you on how to cope with it, reduce the symptoms of it, and show you ways to cure it! All of this info will be coming from our many sources that are verified and highly trustworthy!
